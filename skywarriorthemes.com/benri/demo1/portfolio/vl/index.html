"Chase the vision, not the money, the money will end up following you."


EDC TIET introduces Venture Lab (VL), an event to spread the entrepreneurial mindset to empower students with the tools needed to become the next generation of innovators and changemakers.

VL is a persuasive means to inculcate ideas among students so that they are able to make a profitable, marketable, feasible business out of it in the current orthodox environment. As a result, through the VL workshop, EDC is able to prepare students for collaboration with investors and helps them to sustain in the challenging environment.
